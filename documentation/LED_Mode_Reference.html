LEDModeReference

7-Segment Indicator
Glove MIDI Controller
Rev. — Opus build
Sheet 01 / 01

The strip carries seven RGBW LEDs, numbered 6 → 0 in physical order: LED 6 at the pinky end, LED 0 at the index end. Each mode repaints the strip with a distinct meaning. Every mode below is drawn at a representative state; unlit segments are dark, lit segments in their true colour, each ringed by a 1px outline. The W (white) channel, where used, shows as an inner white core.

01 — Default

Note Play

ALWAYS ACTIVE
when no other mode owns the strip

Touching a finger pad lights its knuckle LED red with a faint white core. On release the LED crossfades to the note’s ripple colour then fades out, while a colour ripple radiates outward.

08 — Indicator · 2 s

Battery Level

SHOWN ON
boot · or IMU double-tap

Green bars from LED 6 show charge (2–7 bars). A low battery blinks LED 6 red. While charging, LED 6 turns solid blue regardless of level. Works whether or not BLE is connected.
